Welcome Augustana Mens Soccer Club

Augustana Universty Mens Soccer club is a compitetive club playing home and away games on and off campus. The club is a member of Great Plains Soccer Conference. Augustana has had a new form of leadership as a new coach joins the team in the season of 2017/2018. Coach John Pagone has made progress by winning the first game of the season here at home.
